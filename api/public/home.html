How We Use Your Data

We take your privacy seriously. Here's what data we collect and why:

Gmail Access

We request access to read your Gmail messages to provide email monitoring and notification services. We never store the content of your emails, only metadata needed for functionality.

Google Sheets Access

We request access to your Google Sheets to enable spreadsheet automation features. We only access the specific sheets you choose to work with.
