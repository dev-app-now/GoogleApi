Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

GGTool collects and processes the following information:

  • Your email address for account creation and authentication
  • Gmail account access tokens (with your explicit permission)
  • Google Drive and Sheets access tokens (with your explicit permission)
  • Basic email metadata for displaying your Gmail messages
  • Google Sheets data for reading and updating spreadsheets

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Gmail monitoring and notification services
  • Enable Google Sheets management and automation
  • Authenticate your access to our services
  • Improve and maintain our services

3. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

  • Secure password hashing
  • JWT-based authentication
  • HTTPS encryption
  • Secure token storage

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication
  • Gmail API for email services
  • Google Drive API for file management
  • Google Sheets API for spreadsheet operations

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Revoke Gmail and Drive access
  • Update your information
